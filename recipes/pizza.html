Pizza Recipe


Description

Savor the authentic taste of Italian cuisine with this delectable Apizza recipe, boasting a crisp yet tender crust and a harmonious blend of fresh toppings, baked to perfection in a traditional wood-fired oven.


Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Optional toppings: Pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, etc.

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature, preferably 500°F (260°C), with a pizza stone or baking sheet inside to preheat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness, keeping it round or forming it into the desired shape.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a pizza peel or the back of a baking sheet dusted with cornmeal or flour.
  4. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Tear the fresh mozzarella into pieces and distribute them evenly over the sauce.
  6. Add any additional toppings you desire, such as pepperoni, mushrooms, or bell peppers.
  7. Grate Parmesan cheese over the top, followed by a drizzle of olive oil and a sprinkle of salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Carefully transfer the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven.
  9. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a minute before slicing.
  11. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, slice, and serve hot. Enjoy your delicious homemade apizza!
