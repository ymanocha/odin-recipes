French Fries Recipe



Description

Indulge in crispy, golden perfection with this irresistible French fries recipe, boasting tender interiors and a delightful crunch on the outside, seasoned to perfection for an addictive snack or side dish


  • Potatoes (preferably russet)
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt
  • Optional seasonings: Paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, etc.

Steps

  1. Wash and peel the potatoes, then cut them into evenly-sized fries, about 1/4 to 1/2 inch thick.
  2. Place the cut fries in a large bowl and cover them with cold water. Let them soak for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours, to remove excess starch.
  3. Drain the soaked fries and pat them dry thoroughly with paper towels to remove any excess moisture.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 325째F (160째C).
  5. Carefully add the dried fries to the hot oil in batches, ensuring not to overcrowd the fryer or pot.
  6. Fry the potatoes for about 3-4 minutes, or until they are just beginning to soften but have not yet developed color.
  7. Remove the partially cooked fries from the oil and place them on a wire rack or paper towels to drain excess oil.
  8. Increase the temperature of the oil to 375째F (190째C).
  9. Once the oil is hot, return the partially cooked fries to the fryer or pot in batches and fry them again until they turn golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes.
  10. As the fries finish cooking, transfer them to a clean wire rack or paper towels to drain.
  11. While the fries are still hot, season them generously with salt and any additional seasonings of your choice, such as paprika, garlic powder, or black pepper.
  12. Toss the fries gently to evenly distribute the seasonings.
  13. Serve the French fries hot and crispy alongside your favorite dipping sauces, and enjoy the irresistible crunch and flavor!
